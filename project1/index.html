Circles vs. Squares

Controls

  • Use wsad or arrow keys to move
  • Use mouse to shoot

Directions

Don't get hit by the square or you will lose a life. Lose all your lives and you lose points.

Shoot the square to make it lose health. If you kill the square, you gain points and move up a level.

Use the points to disadvantage the square or advantage yourself. Everything costs 1 point.

The square increases speed and health after each level.
